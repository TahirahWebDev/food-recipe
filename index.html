Biryani

A flavorful and aromatic rice dish cooked with spices, rice, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (or vegetables)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala, and cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Layer partially cooked rice on top, then add coriander and mint leaves.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Serve hot.
